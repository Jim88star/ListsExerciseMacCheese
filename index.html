Mac n Cheese

You Will Need:

  • 1 1-lb pkg Pasta
  • 1/2 C Butter
  • 1/2 C Flour
  • 2 C Milk
  • 1 tsp Salt

How To:

  1. In a large pot, cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. While pasta cooks, make the cheese sauce:
    1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter.
    2. slowly add flour and salt, stirring constantly until smooth and bubbly.
    3. slowly add milk and bring to a boil sirring constantly until thick.
    4. Remove from heat and add cheese, stirring until blended.
  3. Combine cheese sauce with pasta and enjoy!
