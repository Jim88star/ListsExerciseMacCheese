Mac n Cheese

I love this stuff and it's so easy! As a maindish or a side, it helps make an ideal comfort food meal. Feel free to add bacon, sliced green onions, or anything else you might like.

What You Will Need:

  • 1 1-lb pkg Pasta
  • 1/2 C Butter
  • 1/2 C Flour
  • 2 C Milk
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 lb Cheese
